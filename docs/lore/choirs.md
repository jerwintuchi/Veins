# The Choirs

> **Status:** Drafted (canon framework; the specific Choirs below are proposals).
> **See also:** [../lore.md](../lore.md) · [collegium.md](collegium.md) · [bestiary-fiction.md](bestiary-fiction.md)

## Purpose

The Choirs are the historical generations and doctrinal lineages within the
Collegium. They are **heritage and worldview, never playable classes**. A scholar's
Choir colors how they were taught to read the decay, not what stats they carry.

## Design Philosophy

Because no one holds the complete answer to what an Incarnate is, the Collegium has
fractured over centuries into **Choirs**: lineages that each canonized a different
doctrine of interpretation. Each Choir is a generation's best attempt to explain the
phenomena, hardened over time into dogma and inherited by those who follow. They
disagree, sometimes bitterly, and no Choir is correct. Their disagreement is the
order's intellectual engine and the reason the Archive is contested rather than settled.

A Choir gives a scholar:
- an inherited **doctrine** (what they were taught Incarnates *are*),
- a tradition of **rite and method** (how their lineage prefers to witness, contain, or redeem),
- and a posture in the Collegium's politics.

It does **not** give a class, a skill tree, or a stat block. Roles in play still
emerge from the shared loadout (see [../systems/loadout-economy.md](../systems/loadout-economy.md)),
not from Choir membership.

### Candidate Choirs (proposals, not yet canon)

Three example lineages, each mapped to one of the cosmology's interpretive schools
(see [bestiary-fiction.md](bestiary-fiction.md)). The Director should rename, cut, or add.

- **The Penitential Choir** — holds that Incarnates are *sin made flesh*, the world's
  judgment given a body. Their method leans toward penance, banishment, and redemption.
- **The Reliquary Choir** — holds that Incarnates are *broken holy things bleeding
  sanctity*, relics whose containment failed. Their method leans toward containment,
  warding, and recovery of the relic.
- **The Doxic Choir** — holds that Incarnates are *congealed belief*, a "plague of
  meaning" with no divine author. Their method leans toward observation, measurement,
  and dispassionate classification.

## Non-negotiable Rules

1. Choirs are heritage and doctrine, never classes or power loadouts.
2. No Choir holds the correct answer; the text never crowns one.
3. A Choir's doctrine shapes flavor, dialogue, and preferred method, not raw capability.

## Implementation Notes

- Choir is a cosmetic and narrative attribute on a persisted account (TD-006), in the
  same thin layer as identity and customization. It must never become a stat modifier.
- A Choir's doctrine can flavor **how its scholars annotate the Archive** and the tone
  of their Field Testaments, without changing the underlying facts recorded.
- The three candidate doctrines are deliberately the three Manifestation families from
  the cosmology, so Choir lore and the bestiary stay in lockstep.

## Future Expansion

- Real names and histories for the Choirs once the Director sets the world's proper nouns.
- Inter-Choir politics: schisms caused by a contested classification.
- Cosmetic identity per Choir (heraldry, vestments) in the customization layer.
