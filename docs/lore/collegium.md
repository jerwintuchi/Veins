# The Collegium

> **Status:** Drafted (canon framework; proper nouns open).
> **See also:** [../lore.md](../lore.md) · [choirs.md](choirs.md) · [../gameplay.md](../gameplay.md)

## Purpose

Who the players belong to and why they have the right to do this work. The Collegium
is the institution that issues contracts, keeps the Archive, and sends hunter-
scholars into the decay.

## Design Philosophy

### What it is

The Collegium is **the last attempt to understand reality**. It is an old order of
hunter-scholars: part church, part academy, part field expedition. Its members do
not descend for power or loot; they descend because the world is coming apart at its
sacred seams and someone has to read it before it is gone.

### Why it has the authority

The Collegium's right to confront these phenomena is **historical, not magical**.
The intuition is the Catholic one: for a possession you do not send for a pastor,
you send for a priest, and the priest's standing comes from a long, unbroken
relationship with prior events and accumulated rite. The Collegium is that priest at
the scale of an institution. Centuries of recorded encounters, confirmed
classifications, and survived expeditions are its credential. It is trusted with the
decay because it is the only body that has kept faith with the record of it.

> Authority by lineage and record, not by superpower. A scholar's power is what the
> Archive remembers, not what they can personally channel.

### Its purpose

To **understand in order to contain or redeem**, never simply to kill. Killing an
Incarnate is sometimes the only available mercy, but it is a failure of
understanding, not a trophy. The order's stated aims, in tension with one another,
are to *witness* (record the truth of a thing), to *contain* (keep the decay from
spreading), and where possible to *redeem* (resolve a Manifestation back toward the
sacred order rather than merely ending it).

Candidate sayings for the order (Director to choose or replace):
- "We do not slay what we have not understood."
- "Understanding is the last rite."
- "Witness, contain, redeem; in that order."

### The Archive and contracts

The **Archive** is the Collegium's contested, growing body of confirmed records, the
institutional memory of every classification ever defended. In fiction it is vast and
old; in play it is the party's session-scoped working subset (see TD-006). A
**contract** is the order dispatching scholars to witness, contain, or redeem a
specific reported Manifestation, with the intel the Archive can offer (partial, and
sometimes wrong, because the record itself is interpretation).

## Non-negotiable Rules

1. The Collegium's authority is historical and recorded, never a granted superpower.
2. Its purpose is understanding first; killing is a last resort and a failure, not a goal.
3. The order is not monolithic: its members belong to [Choirs](choirs.md) that disagree.
4. The Archive is fallible. Its records are defended interpretations, not certainties.

## Implementation Notes

- The Collegium frames the **pre-expedition** stage of [../gameplay.md](../gameplay.md):
  contracts are issued here; the Archive supplies the intel a theory is built on.
- **Collegium Rank** (a persisted account stat, TD-006) is standing within the order:
  it gates access to harder contracts and confers prestige, never raw power.
- "Witness, contain, redeem" maps cleanly to the non-kill **primary verbs**
  (observe, capture, banish, drive off) in [../gameplay.md](../gameplay.md).
